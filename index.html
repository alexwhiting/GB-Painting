About Us

Welcome to Groves & Bodnar Painting, your trusted local painting company run by two hardworking university students! We're passionate about delivering high quality, affordable painting services.

Founded by Grant Groves and Isaac Bodnar, we started this business to bring reliable and professional painting services to our community. No matter what the job may be, we take pride in our work and commitment to customer satisfaction.

As university students, we understand the importance of fair pricing, so we keep our rates reasonable without sacrificing quality.
